Week 1

Monday

What did I learn and why?

I duplicated my website to allow me to progress from my prototype website and compare its development at a later stage. I also learnt about colour theory which will advise colours of my own site. In audio, we began practising how to use the equipment.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will be able to change colours of my own website to allow appropriate contrast and readability. In audio I will be confident to use the equipment when recording our audio product.

Tuesday

What did I learn and why?

I learnt how to change alternative text and how this and other methods will make my website more accessible. In audio, we were taught about interview techniques. I contacted my interviewee to organise a date and time to record our audio piece and also pursued further research on our subject to inform our script.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will be able to implement alt text using Atom to make my site more accessible. Interview techniques will mean that I have a clear idea of a structure to use when scripting my own interview.

Wednesday

What did I learn and why?

I researched and audited websites based on their accessibility using criteria learnt on Tuesday. I also completed a quiz on colour theory to consolidate my learning. For photography, I found an online tutorial on how to colour pop elements and applied this technique to my own images by using Photoshop.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

By looking at good and bad website examples I will be able to have a comparison for my own site and adapt it accordingly to increase its accessibility. Photoshop techniques will allow me to enhance my images to make them more captivating for website visitors.

Thursday

What did I learn and why?

I used Photoshop to adjust the size of my images to make them appropriate for my website before integrating them using Atom. In audio, we wrote an intro, outro and questions before meeting and conducting the recording with our interviewee.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

Adjusting the size of my images means that it will take less time for my site to load, making it more accessible. Writing a script and conducting the interview means that we had a structured recording which can be edited and integrated into my site.
